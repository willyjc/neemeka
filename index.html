About Us

NEEMEKA SACCO was established in 1975, is a cooperative society committed to promoting savings, offering affordable credit, and providing financial advisory services. Originally serving Kwale County staff, it has since expanded to include individuals, groups, and companies, helping members manage and grow their finances effectively.

Mission: To enable members to attain financial independence through market-driven products and services.

Vision: To be the model SACCO in offering quality and innovative solutions.

Core Values: Integrity • Teamwork • Customer focus • Professionalism • Equity • Innovation

Savings Products

  • Ordinary Member Account: Min contributions – Individuals Ksh 2,000/month; Groups Ksh 5,000. Annual interest, deposit-based loans. Requirements: form, ID, photo, Ksh 1,000 fee, KRA PIN.
  • Share Capital Account: Member equity, non-withdrawable, transferable. Earns annual dividends.
  • Holiday Savings Account: For festive planning. Min balance Ksh 1,000; monthly min Ksh 500; biannual withdrawals; earns interest.
  • Group & Special Savings: Tailored for groups—standard, deposit, MISLA accounts. Fixed-term savings (3–12 months) with varying interest.
