About Us

NEEMEKA SACCO was established in 1975, is a cooperative society committed to promoting savings, offering affordable credit, and providing financial advisory services. Originally serving Kwale County staff, it has since expanded to include individuals, groups, and companies, helping members manage and grow their finances effectively.
